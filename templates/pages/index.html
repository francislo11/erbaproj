What Is a Home Care?

A warranty for your home is an annual renewable service contract that provides protection and peace of mind, offering a safeguard for when certain essential household systems and appliances break. Home warranties help cut the high cost to repair or replace many of the systems and appliances in your home. They complement your homeowners' insurance by protecting things that aren’t covered under the umbrella of home insurance.

An HomeCare service plan is like a traditional home warranty but better, thanks to its extended coverage and exclusive member perks. The first layer of protection is to help with the cost and hassle that comes with repairing or replacing parts of home appliances and systems that break down over time. But we don’t stop there – our home service plans provide HomeCare members with exclusive pricing on home maintenance services, new appliances, and a variety of home services. From tech installation and setup to pre-season HVAC tune-ups, we’ve got your back.

WHY DO I NEED ONE?

The short answer is that home care coverage is a proactive way to shield the home systems and appliances you rely on every day. It’s a cover-all approach that keeps you protected from the inconvenience of going without the essentials. When an item breaks down due to normal wear and tear, we will help with the cost to repair it. If it can't be repaired, we will replace the item for you (subject to the limitations and exclusions of your plan). It’s not just about the immediate benefits, either – a home service plan helps keep your budget under control and saves you time searching for a qualified contractor.
